About Us

Our team works with the Department of Environment and
Natural Resources, specifically with the Environmental Management Bureau
of the Philippines and we are assigned to monitor the country's waste collection, recycling,
and its proper disposal. Through our collection companies, transfer stations, recycling centers
and landfills, we focus on providing effective and reliable environmental services and
solutions to make proper recycling and waste disposal effortless for the citizens
of this country.
